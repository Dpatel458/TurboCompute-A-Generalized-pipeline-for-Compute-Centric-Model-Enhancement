# Grokking Concept

Grokking is a phenomenon in deep learning where:

- Training accuracy improves early
- Test accuracy improves much later after prolonged training

This delayed generalization is known as grokking.

## Our Contribution

We connect grokking with pruning by:

- Introducing structured sparsity
- Reducing prolonged training requirements
- Encouraging faster convergence

Our approach aims to:
- Reduce unnecessary prolonged training
- Improve efficiency
- Maintain generalization performance
