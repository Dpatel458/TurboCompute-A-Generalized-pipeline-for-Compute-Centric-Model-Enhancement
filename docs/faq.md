# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is KEEP ratio?

KEEP ratio defines how much of the model parameters are retained after pruning.

## Does pruning reduce accuracy?

It may slightly reduce accuracy depending on KEEP ratio.

## Should I retrain after pruning?

Retraining is optional and recommended for small models.

## Do all models require dataset upload?

Only FNN models require dataset upload.

## Is this platform suitable for large transformer models?

Yes, but retraining large models may be computationally expensive.
